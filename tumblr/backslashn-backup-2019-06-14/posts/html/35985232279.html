80 word review: Little Inferno

It is a pleasure to burn: to sit in front of an open fireplace on a cold winter day, watching the flames embrace the fuel, curling around, until it blackens and cracks and is finally nothing but ash. Wood burns. Paper burns. I wonder if this alarm clock will burn? I’ll order everything from the catalogue, just to see if it all burns. I can’t stop. Burn more, even though it’s become a chore. It’s just rote now. Keep burning.

Little Inferno is a toy from Tomorrow Corporation. It is available direct from the developer and on Steam.
