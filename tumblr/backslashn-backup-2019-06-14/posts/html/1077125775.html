Villains are all the same

Karras, to Garrett:

What hast thou built, Garrett? When the time comes to review thy life, with the Master Builder afore thee, and the question is asked, “What hast thou built?”, what will thee say, poor Garrett? For thou'rt a man of destruction, not construction. ‘Tis the biggest sin of all. Build thyself today a good house, that will please him much.

Dr. Breen, to Gordon Freeman:

Tell me, Dr. Freeman, if you can: you have destroyed so much! What is it, exactly, that you have created? Can you name even one thing? I thought not.

Update: Justin Keverne (@JKeverne) pointed me to another instance of this. They must have all been in the same class at villain school.

Andrew Ryan to Jack:

Imagine the will it took to create a place like this. And what have you built? Nothing. You can only loot and break. You’re not a man, you’re just a termite at Versailles.
