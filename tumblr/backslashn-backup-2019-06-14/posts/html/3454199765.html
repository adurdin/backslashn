Beyond Good & Evil is a beautiful game

Beyond Good & Evil is a fabulous, wonderful game. It dates back to 2003, when it was released for the PS2, Xbox, GameCube, and PC. It is being re-released in HD for the Xbox 360 and PS3 this year (March 2nd on XBLA, later on PS3), and is still available for PC from Good Old Games and Steam. The PC version runs happily in at high resolutions, although if you don’t fancy using the mouse and keyboard, you’ll probably want to opt for the console version; apparently the PC version doesn’t support controllers very well.

You really should play this game.
